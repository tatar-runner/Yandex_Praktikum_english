# Robocafe opening in Moscow

The market research of public catering establishments was carried out in order to identify promising formats of public catering establishments and provide recommendations that will help make a business decision to launch a new project.

## A task
* Conduct exploratory data analysis
* Determine the format of the new catering establishment
* Give recommendations on the optimal number of seats and location

## Libraries
*Pandas*, *Matplotlib*, *Seaborn*, *Plotly*

## Research results
The format for the new project was proposed and the decision was justified, recommendations were given on capacity and successful areas for opening. Ideas for scaling the project in the future are proposed.
